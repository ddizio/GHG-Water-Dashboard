# My Context

## My Role
I am a Sustainability Director responsible for global sustainability and ESG strategy across the company. I work in the specialty chemicals business.

My team is small. I need tools that are practical and reusable, not just prototypes.

## What I Am Building
- An interactive dashboard that covers GHG emissions data (Scopes 1-3) and water withdrawal for each site and across the company from 2021 through 2025.


## Tools and Files I Use
- Currently, our data is collected and built in excel spreadsheets. Each year, we have Scope 1, 2, and 3 and water data provided to us in spreadsheets, with no mechanism for trending and no interactive ability to see how past, current, and future projects interact with the data.

## How I Want Claude to Work
